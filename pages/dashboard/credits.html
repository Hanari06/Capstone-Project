Knowledge Channel

The Knowledge Channel Foundation, Inc. (KCFI) envisions a world where children and other learners have access to quality basic education. Through K Channel television, the K Channel website -- kchonline.ph, and K Channel on-demand, KCFI is working towards the realization of this dream by delivering quality education materials to the learners in the classrooms, learning centers, and homes.

The Knowledge Channel Foundation, Inc.(KCFI) is a not-for-profit organization that operates the Knowledge Channel (K Channel) and kchonline.ph, the first and only TV and online media tandem primarily focused on the Philippines' basic education curriculum. Following the Department of Education curriculum as guide, KCFI brings K-12 and Alternative Learning subjects and concepts to life on TV, the Internet and video on demand in the classrooms, learning centers, and homes.
